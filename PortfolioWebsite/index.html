About Me

Securing Systems,

I'm an Information Security Analyst with a strong foundation in penetration testing, network defense, vulnerability assessment, and digital forensics. I hold a degree in Information Science with a Cybersecurity concentration from the University of Pittsburgh.

My work spans both offensive and defensive security — from exploiting vulnerabilities in controlled lab environments to configuring and hardening enterprise networks. Outside of cybersecurity, I pursue game design as a creative outlet, blending technical problem-solving with design thinking.
